17th Avenue Business Park

Tauranga

AWARD-WINNING ‘TROPHY BUILDING’

WE PROMISED IT WOULD BE READY ON 6 SEPTEMBER AND THAT’S THE DAY WE HANDED OVER THE KEYS.

When the ANZ Business Centre opened in 2012, it was deemed a trophy building for Tauranga. This prominent, award-winning building, on the corner of Cameron Road and Elizabeth Street, was the largest commercial office development undertaken in the city for 35 years.

CBC Construction’s ability to secure key tenants made the project fly. Following construction, CBC managed the high end integrated fit-out of all tenancies including KPMG and Staples Rodway accountancy firms, ANZ Bank, Cooney Lees Morgan law firm, Bayleys Real Estate and Elizabeth Café & Larder. Even with the additional fit-out work, the building was finished on time and on budget.

Project Details

  • Value: $22 million
  • Four levels plus basement parking
  • 10,000 m2 office with blue chip tenants
  • Award of excellent at Property Industry Awards 2013
  • Glass-panelled façade with louvre features.
