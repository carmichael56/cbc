Drew Beekie

MANAGING DIRECTOR CBC CONSTRUCTION

They say a company’s ethos evolves from the top. Naturally, managing director Drew Beekie epitomises CBC Construction’s attitude to excellence and integrity.

“Reputation counts for everything in this business,” he says. “It’s essential we look after our customers. I don’t care whether it’s a $5000 job or a $25 million one, if there’s an issue I’ll personally sort it out. The way I see it, it’s my name on the line, too.”

Drew is founder and part owner of CBC Construction. He has a degree in quantity surveying and broad management experience in the industry from market-leading companies in the United Kingdom and New Zealand.

“At CBC, we tackle complex projects and still deliver on time and on budget. I’m proud of that. And I always get a kick out of delivering the finished building to a happy customer.”

MATTHEW LAGERBERG

GROUP DIRECTOR CBC & CLASSIC BUILDERS

Since the launch of CBC, Matthew has been a pivotal figure in its early development. Over time he’s used his business acumen to assist Drew in guiding CBC towards being the very successful company it is today. He has managed significant elements of CBC including, tendering and overseeing major construction works, to name a few.

As CBC has grown, Matthew’s role has shifted and his technical strengths now see him taking a strategic technical role within the group. His expertise offers a valuable sounding board for any technical issues or aspects of construction needing professional consultation or advice.

PETER COONEY

GROUP DIRECTOR CBC CLASSIC BUILDERS

Peter has established and run the Classic Builders Group of Companies from its inception in 1996 to the present day. In more recent years, he has taken on a business development role, and spends much of his time negotiating property acquisition opportunities along with commercial and industrial business propositions.

Over almost 20 years, Peter has built up positive relationships with local authorities and the business community, and is a vital part of the CBC leadership team.
