About

CBC CONSTRUCTION – CAPABILITY WITH CARE

Since its inception in 2006, CBC Construction has become a highly-respected face within New Zealand’s commercial construction industry.

Major developments continue to encompass the retirement, education and healthcare sectors, as well as industrial and commercial facilities.

Equal care is devoted to every customer, whether they seek construction of a multi-storey premium apartment complex or need to fit-out the interior of a more modest retail space.

REPEAT CUSTOM

Our customers have good reason to trust us with their projects, then return to us again and again. CBC Construction completes projects on time and on budget, while meeting every required quality standard.

We are involved in all aspects of commercial construction beginning with financing, land identification, property negotiation and resource consent applications.

We also manage planning, architecture, construction delivery and all-important after-build care because no job is finished until the customer is content. This is what keeps our customers coming back.

UNCOMMON CARE

Our Tauranga-based firm is an integral part of the Classic Group, which encompasses a residential building business, a land-development business and of course CBC Construction with its focus on commercial property.

While CBC Construction stands firmly on its own merits, it does enjoy access to the broader infrastructure and expertise within the wider Classic Group.

Naturally, CBC Construction built the company’s 700m2 purpose-built office space. Our proven systems and rigorous

attention to detail have cemented a reputation for uncommon capability, from the earliest planning stages through to final finishing touches.

So we deliver results with integrity. Every time.
